About

Under the aegis of ACM, the student chapter of AITR is formed with the aim of making current students, future professionals.


AITR-ACM plans to host a wide range of events, including technical workshops, guest lectures and networking to connect the students and experts across the computing spectrum and to stay updated with the latest trends in the technical-research world.

FAQs

What is the participation fee?

Just some enthusiasm and an open mind are enough! We don't charge any money!

How do I register for ACM?

Click on the "Register Now" button in menu and join us. For any further information, feel free to contact us.

Who all can participate?

Everyone can participate! If you are enthusiastic to level up your skills, then you are most welcome! But yes, you need to commit an year for the program!

Why should I participate in ACM?

Our main aim is to bridge the gap between our members and technology. We conduct many seminars and events to make learning easy and fun! From learning about latest technologies by resources provided by the core team to an excellent opportunity to network with like-minded people, we help students get it!

Being a part of this program, what all opportunities will come your way?

You'll get a chance to explore new technologies and eventually will expand your knowledge and interest!
